                                        Reading Passage

1. What is the main role of an operating system?
    >>The main role of an operating system is to manage hardware resources and provide services for computer programs. It acts as an interface between the user and the hardware.
2. Why is memory management important?
    >>Memory management is important because it ensures efficient use of memory, prevents conflicts between programs, and improves overall system performance.
3. What is process scheduling?
    >>Process scheduling is the method used by the operating system to decide which process runs at what time, ensuring efficient CPU utilization and smooth multitasking.
