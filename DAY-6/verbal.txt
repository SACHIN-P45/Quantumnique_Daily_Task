                                            BLOCK 2 – VERBAL ABILITY

1. Why are databases important in modern applications?
    >>Databases are important because they store, manage, and retrieve data efficiently, which helps applications run smoothly and handle large amounts of data.

2. What does data consistency mean?
    >>Data consistency means that the data remains accurate, reliable, and the same across the database without errors or conflicts.

3. Why do companies need well-designed databases?
    >>Companies need well-designed databases to improve performance, ensure data accuracy, and support scalable and efficient software systems.
