                                    BLOCK 3 – COMMUNICATION


One challenge I faced while learning coding was understanding programming logic, especially while solving problems. In the beginning, I found it difficult to think step by step and convert ideas into code. Even simple problems took a lot of time for me.
The main reason it was difficult is because coding requires logical thinking and practice. I was also not very strong in English communication, which made it harder to understand concepts clearly.
To overcome this, I started practicing daily. I solved simple problems first and gradually moved to more complex ones. I also watched tutorials, read explanations, and practiced writing code regularly. Slowly, I started understanding patterns and logic.
From this experience, I learned that consistency and practice are very important in coding. I also learned not to give up when facing difficulties. Now, I feel more confident in solving problems and improving my skills.
