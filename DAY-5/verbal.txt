                            Reading Passage Answers
                            
1. Why are algorithms important?
    >>Algorithms are important because they help solve problems efficiently. They optimize system performance by reducing execution time and minimizing the use of resources like memory and processing power.

2. What happens if inefficient algorithms are used?
    >>If inefficient algorithms are used, the system becomes slow and consumes more resources. This can lead to poor performance, increased costs, and a bad user experience.

3. What is optimization in computing?
    >>Optimization in computing means improving the efficiency of a program or system by reducing time complexity and resource usage while maintaining correct output.
